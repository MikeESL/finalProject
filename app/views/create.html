Create Your Ghost Story

There are eight parts to creating your ghost story! Don't worry, it's painless. By breaking your story up into eight parts, we hope to be sure all possible information is included. Take your time, be creative, and even look at other posts before you begin. Thanks for using ghostTracker! Have fun!

1.

Please title your eperience (be creative!)

2.

In what city or town did this take place?

3.

Where in the the city did this happen (specific location, building, address, etc)?

4.

Date of your experience?

6.

How many people experienced or witnessed this event?

7.

Did you physically feel anything (drop in temperature, did something touch you, etc)? Please briefly describe (limited to 150 characters)

8.

Add a brief overall description of what happened (limited to 350 characters)
